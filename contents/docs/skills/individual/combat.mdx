## How to Level Up

Combat experience is gained by fighting enemies and monsters in dangerous areas throughout Stepcraft.

## Enemy Types

| Type | Description | Difficulty | Common Locations |
|------|-------------|------------|------------------|
| **Slimes** | Beginner-friendly enemies perfect for new players learning combat mechanics | Easy | [Hollow Crown](/docs/map/individual/hollow_crown) |
| **Mushrooms** | Basic enemies that require basic combat skills and decent equipment | Easy | [Hollow Crown](/docs/map/individual/hollow_crown) |
| **Rats** | Intermediate enemies requiring upgraded equipment and tactical awareness | Medium | [Thornspire Ruins](/docs/map/individual/thornspire_ruins) |
| **Ents** | Intermediate enemies requiring upgraded equipment and tactical awareness | Medium | [Hollow Crown](/docs/map/individual/hollow_crown) |
| **Ghosts** | Challenging enemies that demand high-quality gear and combat experience | Medium | [Thornspire Ruins](/docs/map/individual/thornspire_ruins) |
| **Liches** | Formidable foes requiring end-game equipment and advanced strategies | Hard | [Sunforge Sands](/docs/map/individual/sunforge_sands) |
| **Golems** | Elite enemies that challenge even veteran players with advanced gear | Hard | [Sunforge Sands](/docs/map/individual/sunforge_sands) |

See **[Enemies](/docs/enemies)** for detailed information on individual creatures.
