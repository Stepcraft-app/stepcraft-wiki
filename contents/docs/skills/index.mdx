## Leveling Data

- **Updated September 2025** - Formula revised based on community data  
- Levels without a `*` have been confirmed by the community.  
- Levels with a `*` are still unconfirmed estimates.  
- If confirming level data, please remove the * in the Level Range column

### Leveling Formula

The current leveling formula is: **XP(L→L+1) = round(49.9 × 1.107^L)**

- **Accuracy:** Verified against 34 community-confirmed levels with 0.038% average error.
- **XP to Next Level**: Experience points needed to advance from current level to next level
- **Difference**: Increase in XP compared to the previous level-up  
- **Total XP**: Cumulative XP needed to reach that level from level 0
