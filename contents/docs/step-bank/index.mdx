---
title: Step Bank
description: Storage pool that saves unused steps for later use in activities.
keywords: [step bank, steps, storage, agility]
---


The Step Bank is a storage pool for steps that are not being used right away.

If you are not doing an activity, or if an activity finishes and there are leftover steps, those steps are saved in the Step Bank instead of being lost. Later, when you start walking again, the game can use those stored steps to help your current activity progress faster.

The Step Bank has a limited size. It starts at `5,000` steps, and the maximum increases by `1,000` for each Agility level.

You can see your current Step Bank and its maximum capacity on the Character screen.
