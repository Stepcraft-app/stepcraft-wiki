## Quest Description

Weary travelers seek hearty meals after long journeys.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Comfort Food] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific comfort food** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Comfort Foods
- [Mushroom Skewer](/docs/items/individual/mushroom_skewer), [Cooked Bird](/docs/items/individual/cooked_bird), [Drumstick](/docs/items/individual/drumstick), [Fish Soup](/docs/items/individual/fish_soup), [Fried Egg](/docs/items/individual/fried_egg)
- [Cookie](/docs/items/individual/cookie), [Cooked Crab](/docs/items/individual/cooked_crab), [Cooked Shrimp](/docs/items/individual/cooked_shrimp), [Bacon](/docs/items/individual/bacon), [Hamburger](/docs/items/individual/hamburger)
- [Hot Dog](/docs/items/individual/hot_dog), [Berry Pie](/docs/items/individual/berry_pie), [Chocolate](/docs/items/individual/chocolate), [French Fries](/docs/items/individual/french_fries), [Omelette](/docs/items/individual/omelette)
- [Spaghetti](/docs/items/individual/spaghetti), [Garlic Bread](/docs/items/individual/garlic_bread), [Popcorn](/docs/items/individual/popcorn), [Salad](/docs/items/individual/salad), [Taco](/docs/items/individual/taco)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Cooking XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
