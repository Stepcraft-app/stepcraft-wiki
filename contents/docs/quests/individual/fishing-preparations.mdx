## Quest Description

The anglers guild needs fresh bait for tomorrow's fishing tournament.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Bait] to Pearlsand Strand Docks (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific bait type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** Pearlsand Strand Docks

### Possible Bait Types
- [Red Bait](/docs/resources/individual/red_bait), [Maggot Bait](/docs/resources/individual/maggot_bait), [Fly Bait](/docs/resources/individual/fly_bait), [Basic Bait](/docs/resources/individual/basic_bait), [Shrimp Bait](/docs/resources/individual/shrimp_bait)
- [Peach Bait](/docs/resources/individual/peach_bait), [Tiger Bait](/docs/resources/individual/tiger_bait), [Puff Bait](/docs/resources/individual/puff_bait), [Half Shoal](/docs/resources/individual/half_shoal), [Sardine Bait](/docs/resources/individual/sardine_bait)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Fishing XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Pearlsand Strand Docks](/docs/map/individual/pearlsand_strand_docks)
