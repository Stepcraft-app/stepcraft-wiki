## Quest Description

A local couple needs beautiful jewelry to complete their wedding ceremony.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Ring] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 5-25 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific ring type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 5-25 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Ring Types
- [Copper Ring](/docs/items/individual/copper_ring), [Tin Ring](/docs/items/individual/tin_ring), [Iron Ring](/docs/items/individual/iron_ring), [Silver Ring](/docs/items/individual/silver_ring), [Gold Ring](/docs/items/individual/gold_ring)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Trinketry XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
