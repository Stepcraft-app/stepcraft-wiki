## Quest Description

Thick furs are needed to keep the village warm through the cold season.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Hide] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific hide type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Hide Types
- [Bear Hide](/docs/resources/individual/bear_hide), [Deer Hide](/docs/resources/individual/deer_hide), [Wolf Hide](/docs/resources/individual/wolf_hide), [Rabbit Hide](/docs/resources/individual/rabbit_hide)
- [Squirrel Hide](/docs/resources/individual/squirrel_hide), [Moose Hide](/docs/resources/individual/moose_hide), [Hog Hide](/docs/resources/individual/hog_hide), [Fox Hide](/docs/resources/individual/fox_hide)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Hunting XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
