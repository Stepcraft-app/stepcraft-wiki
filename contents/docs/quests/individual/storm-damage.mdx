## Quest Description

As you work at the plaza ovens, you overhear worried voices. **Tamion**, the village carpenter, is speaking urgently with neighbors.

**"The storm last night took half my roof! With the festival starting, I can't gather materials and fix it alone."**

Seeing you complete your task, he approaches. **"You there! It's your Giving Days, yes? I need help gathering materials to repair my home before the rains return."**

## Objectives

- Collect 25 Sticks from Titan's Rest
- Collect 20 Stone from Titan's Rest
- Craft 5 Crude Planks at Willowbend Plaza Carpentry Bench
- Craft 10 Stone Bricks at Willowbend Plaza Forge
- Deliver to Tamion at Willowbend Plaza

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- 175 Carpentry XP
- 175 Smithing XP
- 250 Mining XP
- 250 Logging XP
- 500 Reputation XP
- 500 Gold
