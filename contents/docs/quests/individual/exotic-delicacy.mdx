## Quest Description

A traveling merchant seeks rare fruits from distant lands.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Exotic Fruit] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific exotic fruit** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Exotic Fruits
- [Grapes](/docs/resources/individual/grapes), [Coconut](/docs/resources/individual/coconut), [Banana](/docs/resources/individual/banana), [Pineapple](/docs/resources/individual/pineapple), [Apple](/docs/resources/individual/apple), [Apricot](/docs/resources/individual/apricot)
- [Cherry](/docs/resources/individual/cherry), [Chili Pepper](/docs/resources/individual/chili_pepper), [Dragon Fruit](/docs/resources/individual/dragon_fruit), [Kiwi](/docs/resources/individual/kiwi), [Lemon](/docs/resources/individual/lemon), [Orange](/docs/resources/individual/orange)
- [Papaya](/docs/resources/individual/papaya), [Pear](/docs/resources/individual/pear), [Pomegranate](/docs/resources/individual/pomegranate), [Watermelon](/docs/resources/individual/watermelon)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Foraging XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
