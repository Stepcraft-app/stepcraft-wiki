## Quest Description

A collector seeks impressive specimens for display.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Trophy Item] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific trophy item** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Trophy Items
- [Bear](/docs/resources/individual/bear), [Deer](/docs/resources/individual/deer), [Wolf](/docs/resources/individual/wolf), [Moose](/docs/resources/individual/moose)
- [Bird Skull](/docs/resources/individual/bird_skull), [Bird 2 Feather](/docs/resources/individual/bird_2_feather)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Hunting XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
