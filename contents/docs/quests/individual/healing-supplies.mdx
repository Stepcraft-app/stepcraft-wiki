## Quest Description

The infirmary is preparing for winter and needs medicinal herbs or potions.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Medical Supply] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific medical supply** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Medical Supplies
- [Button Mushroom](/docs/resources/individual/button_mushroom), [Fly Agaric](/docs/resources/individual/fly_agaric), [Penny Bun](/docs/resources/individual/penny_bun), [Chanterelle](/docs/resources/individual/chanterelle), [Morel](/docs/resources/individual/morel)
- [Parrot Waxcap](/docs/resources/individual/parrot_waxcap), [Pixies Parasol](/docs/resources/individual/pixies_parasol), [Witchs Hat](/docs/resources/individual/witchs_hat), [Violet Webcap](/docs/resources/individual/violet_webcap)
- [Silver Leaves](/docs/items/individual/silver_leaves), [Pure Water](/docs/items/individual/pure_water)
- [Minor Healing Potion](/docs/items/individual/minor_healing_potion), [Healing Potion](/docs/items/individual/healing_potion), [Greater Healing Potion](/docs/items/individual/greater_healing_potion)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Foraging XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
