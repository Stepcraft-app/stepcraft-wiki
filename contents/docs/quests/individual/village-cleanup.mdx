## Quest Description

Participate in the village cleanup event by recycling trash.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Recyclable] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific recyclable** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Recyclables
- [Barrel](/docs/resources/individual/barrel), [Boot](/docs/resources/individual/boot), [Bottle](/docs/resources/individual/bottle), [Crate](/docs/resources/individual/crate), [Fish Skeleton](/docs/resources/individual/fish_skeleton)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Fishing XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
