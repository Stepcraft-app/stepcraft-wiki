## Quest Description

The village kitchen prepares for distinguished guests.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Gourmet Food] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific gourmet food** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Gourmet Foods
- [Cooked Lobster](/docs/resources/individual/cooked_lobster), [Calamari](/docs/resources/individual/calamari), [Sushi](/docs/resources/individual/sushi), [Sashimi](/docs/resources/individual/sashimi), [Shucked Oyster](/docs/resources/individual/shucked_oyster)
- [Salmowich](/docs/items/individual/salmowich), [Pizza Slice](/docs/items/individual/pizza_slice), [Blue Fish](/docs/items/individual/blue_fish), [Onion Soup](/docs/items/individual/onion_soup)
- [Breakfast](/docs/items/individual/breakfast), [Dinner](/docs/items/individual/dinner), [Crimson Fruit Salad](/docs/items/individual/crimson_fruit_salad)
- [Chocolate Cake](/docs/items/individual/chocolate_cake), [Strawberry Cake](/docs/items/individual/strawberry_cake)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Cooking XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
