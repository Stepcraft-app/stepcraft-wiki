## Quest Description

A noble family has commissioned jewelry requiring rare metals and gems.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Precious Material] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 5-25 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific precious material** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 5-25 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Precious Materials
- [Gold Bar](/docs/resources/individual/gold_bar), [Blue Bar](/docs/resources/individual/blue_bar), [Red Bar](/docs/resources/individual/red_bar)
- [Dustmote Crystal](/docs/resources/individual/dustmote_crystal), [Embercore Ruby](/docs/resources/individual/embercore_ruby), [Moonwell Opal](/docs/resources/individual/moonwell_opal)
- [Goldleaf Peridot](/docs/resources/individual/goldleaf_peridot), [Tidecrest Sapphire](/docs/resources/individual/tidecrest_sapphire), [Voidstone](/docs/resources/individual/voidstone)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Mining XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
