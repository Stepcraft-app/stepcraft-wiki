## Quest Description

The local alchemist requires rare ingredients for important magical research.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Attack Potion] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 5-25 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific attack potion** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 5-25 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Attack Potions
- [Minor Attack Potion](/docs/items/individual/minor_attack_potion), [Attack Potion](/docs/items/individual/attack_potion)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Alchemy XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
