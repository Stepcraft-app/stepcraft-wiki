## Quest Description

The community celebration requires abundant produce.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Crop] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific crop** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-100 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Crops
- [Carrot](/docs/resources/individual/carrot), [Wheat](/docs/resources/individual/wheat), [Egg](/docs/resources/individual/egg), [Flour](/docs/resources/individual/flour), [Milk](/docs/resources/individual/milk), [Sugar](/docs/resources/individual/sugar)
- [Avocado](/docs/resources/individual/avocado), [Broccoli](/docs/resources/individual/broccoli), [Corn](/docs/resources/individual/corn), [Eggplant](/docs/resources/individual/eggplant), [Garlic](/docs/resources/individual/garlic), [Grapes](/docs/resources/individual/grapes)
- [Lettuce](/docs/resources/individual/lettuce), [Onion](/docs/resources/individual/onion), [Potato](/docs/resources/individual/potato), [Peas](/docs/resources/individual/peas), [Radish](/docs/resources/individual/radish), [Tomato](/docs/resources/individual/tomato)
- [Yellow Pepper](/docs/resources/individual/yellow_pepper), [Rice](/docs/resources/individual/rice)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Farming XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
