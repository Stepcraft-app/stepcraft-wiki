## Quest Description

The village pantry needs sweet preserves to last through the winter months.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Jam] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 10-50 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific jam type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 10-50 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Jam Types
- [Apple Jam](/docs/items/individual/apple_jam), [Apricot Jam](/docs/items/individual/apricot_jam), [Banana Jam](/docs/items/individual/banana_jam), [Blueberry Jam](/docs/items/individual/blueberry_jam), [Cherry Jam](/docs/items/individual/cherry_jam)
- [Chili Pepper Jam](/docs/items/individual/chili_pepper_jam), [Dragonfruit Jam](/docs/items/individual/dragonfruit_jam), [Grape Jam](/docs/items/individual/grape_jam), [Kiwi Jam](/docs/items/individual/kiwi_jam), [Lemon Jam](/docs/items/individual/lemon_jam)
- [Orange Jam](/docs/items/individual/orange_jam), [Papaya Jam](/docs/items/individual/papaya_jam), [Pear Jam](/docs/items/individual/pear_jam), [Pineapple Jam](/docs/items/individual/pineapple_jam), [Pomegranate Jam](/docs/items/individual/pomegranate_jam)
- [Raspberry Jam](/docs/items/individual/raspberry_jam), [Strawberry Jam](/docs/items/individual/strawberry_jam)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Cooking XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
