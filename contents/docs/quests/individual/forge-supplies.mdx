## Quest Description

The smithy requires quality metals to craft essential items for the community.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Metal Bar] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 25-100 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific metal bar** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 25-100 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Metal Bars
- [Copper Bar](/docs/resources/individual/copper_bar), [Tin Bar](/docs/resources/individual/tin_bar), [Iron Bar](/docs/resources/individual/iron_bar), [Silver Bar](/docs/resources/individual/silver_bar), [Gold Bar](/docs/resources/individual/gold_bar)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Smithing XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
