## Quest Description

The ancient temple requires skilled carpentry work to restore its former glory.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Workbench] to Willowbend Plaza (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 5-25 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific workbench type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 5-25 range)
- **Delivery location** Willowbend Plaza

### Possible Workbench Types
- [Birch Workbench](/docs/items/individual/birch_workbench), [Oak Workbench](/docs/items/individual/oak_workbench), [Pine Workbench](/docs/items/individual/pine_workbench), [Redwood Workbench](/docs/items/individual/redwood_workbench), [Silverleaf Workbench](/docs/items/individual/silverleaf_workbench), [Eldritchwood Workbench](/docs/items/individual/eldritchwood_workbench)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Carpentry XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

[Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza)
