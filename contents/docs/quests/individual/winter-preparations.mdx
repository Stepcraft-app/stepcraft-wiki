## Quest Description

Families gather fuel before the cold season arrives.

## Objectives

- **Deliver:** [Specific Wood Type] to quest location (varies daily)
- **Quantity:** [Specific Amount] (varies between 50-300 based on daily quest)

### Daily Quest Details
When this quest appears, it will specify:
- **One specific wood type** from the possible list below
- **Exact quantity** needed (within the 50-300 range)
- **Delivery location** (same as pickup location)

### Possible Wood Types
- [Sticks](/docs/resources/individual/sticks), [Birch Log](/docs/resources/individual/birch_log), [Oak Log](/docs/resources/individual/oak_log), [Pine Log](/docs/resources/individual/pine_log)
- [Redwood Log](/docs/resources/individual/redwood_log), [Silverleaf Log](/docs/resources/individual/silverleaf_log), [Eldritchwood Log](/docs/resources/individual/eldritchwood_log)

## Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

- **Gold:** 25-100% of the base gold value of items (skewed toward lower end, rounded to nearest 20)
- **Reputation XP:** 25% of gold reward (rounded to nearest 10)  
- **Woodcutting XP:** 50% of XP normally gained from obtaining the items (rounded to nearest 50)

## Found At

Any Job Board Region
