## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~300 HP, 95 ATK, 60 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Ents have high health and defense but move slowly
- Circle around them to avoid their powerful attacks\n- Ensure your equipment is upgraded
- Stock up on healing potions
